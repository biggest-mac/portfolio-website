The focus of her work is...
Wallace is interested in uncomfortable emotional truths and suspicious of conceptions of identity that demand rigid boundaries.
My work reflects my internal state at any given moment; I am much more interested in uncovering my subconscious through the visual associations it makes than trying to meticulously plan and execute a piece. Often, this process requires a lot of Sitting in Rooms and Thinking About Stuff, which doesn't appear very exciting.
